About Us

Welcome to SkyView, where technology meets innovation in the quest to safeguard our oceans. At SkyView, our mission is to provide an automated, accurate, and efficient solution for detecting oil spills in maritime environments. Leveraging advanced AIS (Automatic Identification System) and satellite data, we aim to enhance the safety and sustainability of water navigation. Join us on our journey as we navigate the waters of innovation, turning complex data into actionable insights that help keep our oceans safe.
